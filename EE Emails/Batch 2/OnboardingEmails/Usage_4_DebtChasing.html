Despite your best efforts, some customers may fail to pay you on time.

If this happens, it’s important to get in touch immediately to understand why they haven’t paid and remind them of your credit terms.

It is best to send an email or make a call first, then follow up with a letter to have the best chance of recovering the debt as quickly as possible.

If you’re dealing with small businesses it may just be the case that they’ve forgotten and need a friendly reminder.


 
 
 


Send a written reminder to your customers and provide them with an invoice statement that summarises their outstanding debt.




Store any correspondence you have with a customer regarding overdue payment in CreditHQ. That way, you’ll have an easy-to-find written record, just in case you need it for future reference.
