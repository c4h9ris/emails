Once you’ve agreed to offer credit to a customer, you’ll want to make sure you get paid on time, every time.

CreditHQ enables you to track your correspondence with each customer and monitor their credit rating over time – two simple ways of keeping the trading cycle running smoothly.

 
 
 


Store records of letters, emails or calls you exchange with your customers in CreditHQ so that you can find them easily if you ever need to remind customers of your agreements.




CreditHQ helps to reduce the risk of a trading relationship turning sour by alerting you automatically to any changes to your customer’s credit profile.

You can also keep track of their credit performance by looking at the history in their credit profile, just click on the company name in CreditHQ.
