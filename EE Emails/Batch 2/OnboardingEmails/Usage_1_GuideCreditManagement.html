CreditHQ takes the hassle out of each step of the credit management process, from setting trading terms with customers to tracking their credit background and chasing payment.

Credit management is a process that seeks to reduce the amount of bad debt a company is exposed to. This is achieved initially by evaluating a company or customer for their credit-worthiness and deciding how much credit you want to extend to the company (if any) and for how long – the credit terms.

Once this is done and the company has been invoiced for any work completed, it's best practice to create a tight process for updating companies of their obligations, collecting payment on time and, should a company not pay what they owe, collecting the debt.
